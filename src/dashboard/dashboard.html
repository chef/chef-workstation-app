Chef-workstation-app

The Chef Workstation Application enables you to view your cookbook information across multiple Chef Infra Repositories. Add a local Chef Infra Repository here and manage your cookbooks right from this application. You can upload a cookbook or push a Policyfile to the specified Chef Infra Server without having to execute commands from the Terminal.
